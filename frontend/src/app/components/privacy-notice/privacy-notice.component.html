Data We Store

When you log in, we store the following personal data:

  • Commander (CMDR) name — your in-game Elite Dangerous callsign. Obtained from Frontier Developments at login and from survey spreadsheets you submit. Used to identify and attribute your survey contributions.
  • Frontier Developments customer ID — a numeric identifier from your FDev account. Used solely to ensure account uniqueness and prevent duplicate records.

Legal Basis

  • Login data: contract performance — necessary to provide the service you requested.
  • CMDR names from submitted survey data: legitimate interest — attribution of scientific survey contributions.

Authentication

Login is handled via Frontier Developments' OAuth2 system. Frontier Developments' own privacy policy governs what they collect during authentication.

Session Cookie

A single essential session cookie (edst-auth) is used for authentication. No tracking or analytics cookies are used.

Retention & Your Rights

Your data is retained for as long as you use the service. You may unlink your FDev account via Settings → Delete Account. Your CMDR name is retained afterwards for survey attribution.

To exercise your rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability), please contact us on GitHub.
